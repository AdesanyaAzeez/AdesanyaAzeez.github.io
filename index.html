About Me

Hi! I'm AbdulAzeez.
A Data Analyst and Analytics Engineer with 3+ years of experience designing scalable data systems and transforming complex data into actionable insights that drive measurable business impact. I specialize in building efficient data models, optimizing SQL for performance, and using modern BI tools to uncover patterns that support informed decision-making.

I develop reliable, scalable datasets and data architectures that enable accurate, real-time analysis while maintaining strong data integrity. My work includes optimizing data warehouses, defining KPIs aligned with business goals, and translating them into clear, interactive dashboards that empower stakeholders to make confident decisions.

Beyond my professional work, I mentor aspiring data professionals and take on freelance projects, helping individuals and organizations unlock the full value of their data.
