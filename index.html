ABOUT ME


Hello! I am AbdulAzeez.
I am a Data Analyst with about three(3) years hands-on experience in nurturing data from its raw form to the point of analysis, transforming it into actionable insights that influence business decision making. My career has been a thrilling blend of numbers, insights, problem-solving and cross-functional collaboration, bridging the gap between technical nerds and the business team.

I specialize in using a variety of tools and techniques, including robust business intelligence (BI) platforms, to help businesses transform data into insights. Outside of the corporate world, I teach data analytics, helping data enthusiasts in attaining their full potential and developing in their careers. I also do freelance work, assisting clients in achieving their data objectives.
